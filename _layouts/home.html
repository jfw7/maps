Maps

Nick Rich + Jonathan Wall

Maps is a series of digital transformations of Emily Dickinson's poem "I cross till I am weary". 6 variations on the poem were created using speech recognition software. Each variation uses a distinct source text to "map" the sounds of the original poem onto the closest available phonemes in a new vocabulary.
